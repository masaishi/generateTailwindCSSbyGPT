Features

Multi-Node Orchestration

FLUTE Commerce offers multi-node orchestration, allowing you to train your models on multiple machines for significantly faster training times.

Differential Privacy

FLUTE Commerce allows you to train your models with local or global differential privacy, ensuring that individual customer data is never compromised.

Model Quantization

FLUTE Commerce supports model quantization, allowing you to reduce the size of your models for faster inference times.
