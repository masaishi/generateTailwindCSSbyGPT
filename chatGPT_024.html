Features

Skin tone analysis

SmartMirror uses machine learning algorithms from the Arm Compute Library to analyze a user's skin tone and suggest the perfect make-up shade.

Make-up product scanning

Users can scan make-up products to see how it would look on their skin.

Personalized suggestions

SmartMirror provides personalized make-up suggestions based on the user's skin tone and preferences.

Compatibility with different skin types

SmartMirror is compatible with different skin types, including dry, oily, and sensitive skin.

Offline use

SmartMirror can be used offline, making it convenient for users who don't have access to the internet.

Advanced optimization techniques

SmartMirror uses advanced optimization techniques, such as kernel fusion and texture utilization, to improve performance and reduce power consumption.
