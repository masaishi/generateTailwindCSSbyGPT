Federated Learning

FLUTE Commerce uses federated learning to train models on data owned by individual customers, not shared among competing e-commerce outlets. This ensures maximum customer data privacy while enabling personalized customer experiences.

Machine Learning Models

FLUTE Commerce supports most model types including computer vision, convolutional neural networks, recurrent neural networks, and Huggingface Transformers. This ensures that your e-commerce outlet can develop algorithms that capture the essence of your business and the experience your consumers desire.

Data Privacy and Security

FLUTE Commerce offers local and global differential privacy, multi-node orchestration, and a variety of standard optimizers and aggregation methods. This ensures that your business can focus on creating personalized customer experiences without worrying about data privacy and security.
