Unleash the Power of Machine Learning with mzutils!

Your one-stop toolkit for efficient NLP and RL workflows.

mzutils is a comprehensive toolkit designed to streamline various machine learning tasks, with a focus on Natural Language Processing (NLP) and Reinforcement Learning (RL). Our toolkit supports a wide range of datasets and their conversions to different formats, including JSON, CSV, numpy arrays, and tensors. With mzutils, you can simplify data cleaning, preprocessing, visualizing, evaluating, and modeling in your machine learning projects, making your workflow more efficient and effective.

Key Features:

  • Simplify data cleaning and preprocessing
  • Seamlessly convert datasets to multiple formats
  • Enhance data visualization and analysis
  • Streamline the evaluation and modeling process
  • Boost efficiency in NLP and RL workflows
