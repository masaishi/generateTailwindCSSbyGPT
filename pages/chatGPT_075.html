FLUTE Commerce

Personalized E-commerce Experiences with Privacy

Federated Learning

FLUTE Commerce uses federated learning to train models on data that is owned by individual customers and not shared among competing e-commerce outlets. This ensures that data privacy is maintained while still allowing for personalization in the shopping experience.

Model Support

FLUTE Commerce supports most model types like CNNs, RNNs, and Huggingface Transformers. So, you can pick the model that suits your needs best and get started right away.

Variety of Optimizers

FLUTE Commerce supports a variety of standard optimizers, so you can fine-tune your models for optimal performance.

Multi-Node Orchestration

FLUTE Commerce supports multi-node orchestration, allowing you to scale your simulations to any size.

Local or Global Differential Privacy

FLUTE Commerce supports both local and global differential privacy, ensuring that data privacy is maintained throughout the simulation process.

Model Quantization

FLUTE Commerce supports model quantization, allowing you to optimize your model for efficiency without sacrificing accuracy.
