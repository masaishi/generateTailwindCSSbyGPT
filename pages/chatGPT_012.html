STDO Video Super-Resolution

Summary

STDO is a new method for high-quality and efficient video super-resolution using spatial-temporal data overfitting. The proposed method leverages the spatial-temporal information to accurately divide videos into chunks, thus reducing the number of chunks and storage requirements while maintaining high quality.

One Business Idea

Create a mobile app that allows users to capture low-resolution videos and upscale them to high resolution using the proposed method.
