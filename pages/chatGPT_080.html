What is pyGAM?

Generalized Additive Models (GAMs) in Python

pyGAM allows for the modeling of non-linear relationships and additivity while maintaining interpretability. This makes it particularly useful for financial institutions and investors who need to build flexible and interpretable models.

Penalized B splines and Link functions

pyGAM utilizes penalized B splines and link functions to build models that are easy to interpret, even when dealing with complex and non-linear relationships in large financial datasets.
