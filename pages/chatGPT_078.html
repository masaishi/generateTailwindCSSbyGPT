What is RiskWizard?

RiskWizard is a tool for building models of risk factors influencing financial outcomes using Generalized Additive Models in Python (pyGAM).

With RiskWizard, financial institutions and investors can better assess and manage risk by predicting financial outcomes and identifying key risk factors and their effects.

Features

Prediction of Financial Outcomes

RiskWizard allows you to build models to predict financial outcomes with pyGAM.

Identification of Key Risk Factors and their Effects

RiskWizard can identify the key risk factors that impact financial outcomes and show their effects on the outcome.

Visualization of Models and Relationships

RiskWizard can visualize the models and relationships between variables to help better understand the factors driving financial outcomes.
