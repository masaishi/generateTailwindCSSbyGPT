Multi-Threaded Tokenization

Rust-Bert's implementation of Hugging Face's Transformers library enables efficient tokenization of large datasets, reducing processing time and increasing throughput.

GPU Inference

Rust-Bert leverages GPU acceleration to provide fast and accurate inference for a wide range of NLP tasks, including translation, summarization, and question answering.

Multiple Models

Rust-Bert supports a variety of pre-trained models, including BERT, GPT-2, and RoBERTa, providing flexibility and versatility for different use cases.
