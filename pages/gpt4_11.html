About MediAI

MediAI is a powerful medical imaging analysis application harnessing the capabilities of GaNDLF, an advanced deep learning framework. We aim to aid medical professionals in diagnostics and treatment planning by offering automated and accurate image analysis solutions. Our goal is to improve patient outcomes and streamline the overall medical imaging process. We believe in the potential of artificial intelligence and machine learning to revolutionize healthcare.
