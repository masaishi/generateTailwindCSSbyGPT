Revolutionizing Exoplanet Detection

Our ExoplANNET algorithm is trained to classify planetary signals in radial velocity data with fewer false positives and a faster execution time than traditional methods.

Customizable for Specific Research Needs

Our algorithm can be customized to fit specific research needs and data sets, allowing for more personalized and accurate results.

User-Friendly Interface
