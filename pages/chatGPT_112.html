FAQs

What is GRU-HSIC network?

+

GRU-HSIC network is a new Gate Recurrent Unit (GRU) network based on Hilbert-Schmidt Independence Criterion (HSIC) for accurate state-of-health (SOH) estimation of batteries. It can address the issue of redundant information within the network, which impacts the accuracy of SOH estimation.

What is SOH?

+

State-of-health (SOH) is the current health status of a battery, which indicates its ability to deliver its specified performance for a given application.

What are the benefits of accurate SOH estimation?

+

Accurate SOH estimation can help in ensuring safe and reliable use of batteries in electric vehicles, ships, and aircraft, by predicting their remaining useful life and avoiding unexpected failures.
