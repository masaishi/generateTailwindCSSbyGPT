Application Overview

An AI-powered support system that helps radiologists process and analyze medical imaging data using the GaNDLF framework, leading to faster and more accurate diagnoses.

  • Key features and benefits: detailed reports and analytics, quick diagnosis, ease of use and integration, support for a range of medical imaging data sets.
  • Unique selling points (USPs): RadAI helps radiologists quickly identify anomalies in images with consistent high precision, which helps them diagnose quickly and accurately, reduce diagnosis time and increases throughput.
  • Supported platforms: Desktop, mobile, web
  • Pricing and plans: Subscription model based on the number of cases and amount of data processed.
