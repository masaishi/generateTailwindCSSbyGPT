How FLUTE Commerce Works?

1. Collect Customer Data

FLUTE Commerce enables the collection of customer data from individual customers on their devices, and not shared among competing e-commerce outlets.

2. Train Personalized Models

FLUTE Commerce uses federated learning to train models on the collected data, enabling e-commerce companies to develop machine learning models that can provide personalized customer experiences.

3. Ensure Data Privacy

FLUTE Commerce ensures customer data privacy by using differential privacy and model quantization, preventing the sharing of customer data across e-commerce outlets.
