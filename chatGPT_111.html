SafeCharger

SafeCharger is a service that accurately estimates the state-of-health of your electric vehicle battery, ensuring safe and reliable driving. Our cutting-edge technology uses a GRU-HSIC network to compress information from hidden layers, resulting in higher accuracy than other recurrent models. With real-time battery health monitoring and notification of battery replacement time, you can drive with confidence.

Real-time Monitoring

SafeCharger constantly monitors the health of your electric vehicle battery, providing you with real-time updates on its condition.

Notification of Replacement Time

SafeCharger notifies you when it's time to replace your battery, ensuring that you always have a reliable and safe driving experience.

Driving Range Prediction

SafeCharger predicts your driving range based on your battery's health, helping you plan your trips with ease.
