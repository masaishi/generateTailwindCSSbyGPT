Automated Subject Indexing with Annif

Annif is a statistical automated indexing tool that uses natural language processing and machine learning to automatically assign subject headings to text documents.

What is Annif?

Annif is a tool that helps automate the process of assigning subject headings to documents, which can save significant time and resources. It is based on statistical models and uses natural language processing and machine learning to categorize documents into subject areas.

Features and Benefits

  • Automated subject indexing
  • Improved accuracy and consistency
  • Saves time and resources
  • Customizable training data
  • Open source software

Integrations and Compatibility

Annif can be used with a variety of document management systems and platforms, including:

  • DSpace

  • Invenio
  • Koha
  • Omeka
  • VuFind

Pricing and Packages

Annif is open source software and is available for free. However, support and consulting services are available for a fee.

  • Can Annif be trained on custom data? Yes, Annif can be trained on user-provided training data to improve its accuracy and relevance.
  • Is Annif compatible with my document management system? Annif is compatible with a variety of document management systems and platforms. Please refer to the Annif documentation for more information.
  • How accurate is Annif? The accuracy of Annif depends on the quality and quantity of training data provided. However, in general, Annif has been shown to achieve high levels of accuracy in subject indexing tasks.
