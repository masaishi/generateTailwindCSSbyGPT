SemDeDup

Data-Efficient Learning through Semantic Deduplication

Unique Value Proposition

SemDeDup is a new method for data-efficient learning through semantic deduplication, which can remove semantically similar but not identical data pairs to make models learn faster with less data. It uses pre-trained embeddings to identify and remove semantic duplicates in web-scale datasets, thereby preserving performance and speeding up learning.

Tagline

"Learn Faster with Less Data"

Service Overview

SemDeDup can remove 50% of the data with minimal performance loss, effectively halving training time. It also improves performance out of distribution and provides more efficiency gains compared to prior approaches.
