Automated Exoplanet Detection with ExoplANNET

ExoScan uses the power of machine learning to scan and classify exoplanet signals in astronomical data.

Features

Automated Detection

ExoScan uses ExoplANNET, a deep learning algorithm, to automatically detect exoplanet signals in observational data without the need for manual analysis.

Customizable Scanning Parameters

ExoScan allows users to customize scanning parameters, such as signal-to-noise ratio and transit duration, to optimize detection for their specific research goals.

Compatibility

ExoScan is compatible with various types of astronomical data, including radial velocity and transit photometry, making it a versatile tool for exoplanet research.
