The Problem

E-commerce companies want to provide personalized customer experiences but struggle to do so without putting customer privacy at risk.

The Solution

FLUTE Commerce is a platform that helps e-commerce companies develop machine learning models that can provide personalized customer experiences without compromising customer data privacy.

Features

Federated Learning

Train models on data that is owned by individual customers and not shared among competing e-commerce outlets.

Multi-Node Orchestration

Supports large-scale simulations, multi-GPU and multi-node orchestration.

Differential Privacy

Support for local or global differential privacy to provide security for individual-level data.

Model Quantization

Compress model size without sacrificing performance.
