ExoplANNET: A New Way to Discover Exoplanets

Using neural networks to detect exoplanets in observational data

Introducing ExoplANNET

Traditional methods of detecting exoplanets in radial velocity data can be slow and inaccurate, with a high number of false positives. ExoplANNET is a deep learning algorithm that uses neural networks to accurately classify planetary signals in the data, with faster execution times and fewer false positives.

Our Web-Based Platform

We are developing a user-friendly platform that utilizes the power of ExoplANNET to assist astronomers and researchers in detecting exoplanets in their observational data. Our platform provides an intuitive interface for users to upload their data and receive automated analysis using the ExoplANNET algorithm. Users can also compare results obtained using traditional methods with those obtained using ExoplANNET, allowing them to evaluate the effectiveness of the new technology.

Training and Support

We understand that not all users may be familiar with machine learning and neural networks. That's why we provide training and support to ensure that users can fully utilize the platform and take advantage of the benefits of ExoplANNET.
