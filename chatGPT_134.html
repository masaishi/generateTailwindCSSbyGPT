Features and Benefits

Annif provides the following features and benefits:

Automated Subject Indexing

Annif uses natural language processing and machine learning to automatically assign subject headings to documents, saving significant time and resources.

Statistical Models

Annif is based on statistical models that use a combination of supervised and unsupervised machine learning algorithms to categorize documents into subject areas.

Natural Language Processing

Annif uses natural language processing techniques to analyze and understand the content of documents, and then categorizes them into relevant subject areas.

Integrations and Compatibility

Annif is compatible with the following systems:

WordPress Plugin

Annif can be integrated with WordPress using a simple plugin, allowing you to automatically assign subject headings to your blog posts and articles.

Joomla Extension

Annif can also be integrated with Joomla using a simple extension, allowing you to automatically assign subject headings to your articles and pages.

Drupal Module

Annif can also be integrated with Drupal using a simple module, allowing you to automatically assign subject headings to your articles and pages.
