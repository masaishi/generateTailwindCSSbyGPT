Frequently Asked Questions

What is Annif?

Annif is a statistical automated indexing tool that uses natural language processing and machine learning to assign subject headings to text documents. It is based on statistical models that have been trained on large collections of text documents, resulting in high accuracy and relevance. Annif is designed for ease of use and can be easily integrated into your existing workflows and systems.

What are the benefits of using Annif?

Annif can save significant time and resources by automating the process of assigning subject headings to documents. It is based on statistical models and uses natural language processing and machine learning to categorize documents into subject areas. Annif is also flexible and customizable and can be tailored to your specific needs.

How much does Annif cost?

Annif offers a free starter plan with up to 1000 documents per month, as well as a pro plan starting at $99/month and an enterprise plan with custom pricing. See our pricing page for more details.
